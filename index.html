🏠House Rules

No shoes indoors. The floors are 1936 original hardwood — please leave shoes at the door.
Carry luggage, don't roll it. Please carry all wheeled and hard-shell bags inside, never roll them across the floors.
Hands off the houseplants. Please don't move or water them — we've got it covered.
Flush only toilet paper. No feminine hygiene products, baby wipes, or paper towels — the plumbing is original.
No smoking. No pets.
Do not use the fireplace. It's permanently out of service and the chimney is capped. No fires, ever.
No parties. Quiet hours after 10 PM. This protects our neighborhood permit, and our neighbors.

🔌Appliance Guides

Lights & Fans

Ceiling fansLiving room, dining room, and each bedroom have a ceiling fan. The fan and its light run off a remote — on the wall by that room's main light switch, except the master bedroom, where it's on the bedside table.
Living room lightsMain switches for the ceiling lights are by the front door.
Kitchen lightsSwitches are by the east-facing window, and they're dimmable.

Living room

Smart TV (Samsung)Power on with the Samsung remote and press Home for apps. Live TV is the Samsung TV app. Please sign into your own streaming accounts and sign out at checkout.
Burrow sleeper sofaConverts to a bed: take off the large back pillows, then pull the seat out until it folds flat.
A/C — Black+Decker portableFree-standing on wheels with an exhaust hose to the window — please don't remove or kink the hose. Use the panel or remote: Power, Mode (Cool/Fan/Dry), set temp and fan speed.

Kitchen

Coffee — Keurig K-DuoOn the counter next to the Ninja, by the microwave. Single cup or a full carafe. Fill the reservoir, drop in a pod, pick your size, and brew. Pods are on the counter next to the Ninja.
Microwave (Insignia)Enter a time and press Start, or use the one-touch presets.
Toaster oven / air fryer (Ninja Foodi)Pick a Function (Air Fry, Bake, Toast…), set Temp and Time, then Start.
Gas range & oven (Frigidaire)Cooktop is gas — push in the knob and turn to Lite until the burner catches. Oven: press Bake, set the temp, then Start. Please don't run the Self-Clean cycle during your stay.
Dishwasher (Frigidaire)Load, add a detergent pod, pick a cycle (Normal for most loads), and the door panel starts it.
Refrigerator (LG)Water dispenser in the left door; ice is in the freezer. Please leave the temperature at its current setting.

Laundry

Washer (Samsung)Use the eco-friendly laundry sheets in the laundry-room cupboard (one per load — no liquid detergent needed). Run the Quick Wash cycle. Leave the door cracked open slightly when done.
Dryer (Samsung)Run on Normal. Check the lint tray before running, and remove the lint after each use.

Main bedroom

A/C — Midea window unitTouch panel on top or the remote: Power, Mode (Cool/Heat/Auto/Dry/Fan), Timer, Swing. Eco mode saves energy overnight.

Outdoor

Propane grill (Monument Mesa II)Open the lid before lighting. Open the tank valve, then push a burner knob in to ignite and turn it to the right to keep it lit. Close the tank valve when you're done. Outdoor use only. Please put the cover back on once the BBQ has cooled.

🚨Safety & Emergencies

In any emergency, call 9-1-1.
Property address: 8931 S Denker Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90047 (cross streets 89th & Denker).

If you see fire or smoke

  • Get out immediately with everyone; close doors behind you.
  • Call 9-1-1 once you're safely outside, and give the address above.
  • Meet at the assembly point: the boulevard sidewalk in front of the yard, by the lamp post. Don't go back inside.

Evacuation routes

  • Primary: out the front door → down the driveway → sidewalk → assembly point.
  • Secondary: out the back door → driveway → gate → front.

Safety equipment

  • Fire extinguisher: dining area, near the stove.
  • Fire blanket: drawer to the right of the kitchen stove.
  • First-aid kit + flashlights: laundry room cabinet, left of the washer/dryer.

Utility shut-offs (if you need them)

  • Water: out the front door, turn left off the driveway and follow the path until you see the cactus on your left. The shut-off is to the right of the cactus (flip up to shut off).
  • Gas: front of the house, NW corner — take the path, under the arch, valve immediately to the left.
  • Electrical breaker: backyard — out the back door, turn right, past the water heater, turn right.

Nearest hospitals

  • Memorial Hospital of Gardena — 1145 W Redondo Beach Blvd (~4.8 mi, full ER)
  • Harbor-UCLA Medical Center — 1000 W Carson St, Torrance (~11 mi, Level 1 trauma)
